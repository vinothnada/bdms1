Doner Camp in Gampaha

Gampaha at 2013-09-30 to 2013-09-30   Time: 14:00:00 - 17:00:00

The blood donation camp organized by the Prefect board of Bandaranayke College was successfully held on 26 november 2013 from 9.00 am to 1.00 pm in the college main hall to commemorate “2600th Anniversary of the Enlightenment of Lord Buddha”. Board of Prefects achieved more than expected amount of blood. The medical staff of General Hospital of Gampaha, Students and old boys of our school, Teachers, Parents participated to this event to make it a success.

World Blood Donor Day 2014

Colombo at 2013-09-30 to 2013-09-30   Time: 09:00:00 - 17:00:00

WHO has selected Sri Lanka to host the World Blood Donor Day 2014 global event. It is indeed an honer for all the blood donors of the country. Annually the blood collection by NBTS is 350,000 and as of 2012 statistics the contribution by voluntary donors reaches 99%. Replacement system of blood collection by family members and friends of the patient is not recommended by WHO and Sri lanka is in the reach of 100% voluntary blood donations

Dengue fever restrictions lifted in Piliyandala anPiliyandala and Kesbewa

Piliyandala and Kesbewa at 2013-09-01 to 2013-09-01   Time: 08:00:00 - 00:00:00

Piliyandala and Kesbewa residents can now give blood after the removal of temporary restrictions designed to protect the blood supply from a seasonal dengue fever outbreak. Blood Service is now calling on Piliyandala and Kesbewa residents to roll up their sleeves and help save lives.
